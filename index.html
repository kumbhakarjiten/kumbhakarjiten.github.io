About

I am an Integrated PhD student at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research Kolkata (IISER Kolkata) in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics (DMS). My research interest is Control of Partial Differential Equations (PDEs). More specifically, I am working on the Control of fluid flow models, namely, linearized compressible Navier-Stokes equations in one dimension, and related evolution equations, under the supervision of Dr. Shirshendu Chowdhury.
